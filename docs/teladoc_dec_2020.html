What is Teladoc Health?#

Teladoc is a telehealth company, which connects its clients with hospitals and physicians. There are multiple services that Teladoc currently is offering to its clients in more than 175 countries. It uses advanced software technology and AI to offer on-demand remote medical care so that patients could connect at any time with their preferred board-certified and state-certified doctors within minutes.

How does Teladoc generate revenue?#

Teladoc generates revenue by charging monthly or yearly to their subscribers or individual consultation fee. Teladoc expects to have 51 million subscribers by the end of 2020 and 22 million individuals to pay for the consultation fee. According to its 2019 annual report, it has around 3,100 active licensed physicians and nurses.
